About SpeedTest Pro

Our Mission

SpeedTest Pro was created to provide users with the most accurate and reliable internet speed testing experience. We believe everyone deserves to know their true connection speeds without misleading results.

How It Works

Our advanced testing methodology measures your actual download and upload speeds by transferring real data packets to and from our global network of servers. We calculate ping times to give you a complete picture of your connection quality.

The Team

Founded by our team in 2025, we're passionate about internet performance and transparency in connectivity metrics. We're constantly improving our algorithms to ensure you get the most accurate results possible.
